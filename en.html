About the solution

The project consists of delivering a complete traceability solution, which can be integrated into your system, using technologies such as RFID to receive detections of assets, however, it is not limited to this technology, just receiving the minimum information to record the history of a given asset.

We know that it is not enough just to know where a certain asset is geographically positioned in real time. Nowadays, this type of approach is the starting point for a solution with such comprehensiveness.

Segmento - Distribuidores
Segmento - Logística Portuária

With a focus on sustainability, the solution monitors the useful life of assets and ensures automated alerts for supervisors and subordinates to know exactly what must be discarded, strictly respecting their useful life. In the case of animal traceability, the system has triggers to monitor the activity and serves as a security tool, with the control panel being able to configure each type of alert in a simplified manner.

Knowing exactly which assets to monitor, which assets present doubtful behavior, what these idle assets can influence in the organization's day-to-day life: this is possible with Trackin Automation.

Features

Multi Companies

Manage all you asset tracking of all companies in only one system with our quick access feature and shared data reports.

Lifetime Control

In the system, you can control the date of disposal of your assets and to be alerted when the useful life is reached.

Audit System

Take care of your assets through audits with responsible, assets to be analyzed and traceability throughout the analysis process.

Custom Alerts

With Trackin Automation you configure alerts (Call, SMS, Webhook and E-mail) to monitor undue detections in the movement of assets.

Management Reports

At the system, you will have a high number of reports for collecting asset management information for supervisors.

Done for you

Extra data automatically available according to the category and control of specific activities. Example: Bags, Cattle and others.

Scheduled Inventory

Create counting routines programmed through the system and obtain the result of your inventory daily.

Integrations

Your development team will be able to integrate your system (ERP, PCP, etc.) easily through our API and Webhooks.

About Company

Trackin Automation is a Brazilian Startup focused on technological tools for reverse logistics in commercial, industrial and agribusiness areas.

Beginning in 2019, the company entered into partnerships with Chinese equipment manufacturers so that it could start testing and approving possible equipment on the market.

In the first quarter of 2020, the company presented the main solution and also the strategy to develop its own equipment to reduce dependence on foreign markets.
